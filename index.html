Message from Managing Director, Sanjeev Senta

As an organization we are committed to, connected to, and invested in our communities. It is our desire to lead change in the talent management industry by thinking beyond just connecting talent with opportunities. We aim to provide meaningful opportunities for our consultants and clients, in a manner that aligns with our core values. We strongly believe that leadership is a process, not a title. TekWissen leaders are expected to establish direction, create alignment and build commitment across their teams and with their peers. Everyone at TekWissen is seen, heard and valued. My team and I deliver this message, clearly and consistently, with the hope that all TekWissenites will contribute to something bigger than themselves.

We are highly committed to diversity. At TekWissen diversity is inclusion, acceptance, honoring differences, and respecting others. Because we have LGBT+ ownership we focus on developing practices to include more minorities, LGBT+ and women in the workforce. We continually focus on developing new strategies and partnerships to help recruit a larger and deeper pool of diverse candidates for our clients. Our commitment to supporting clients is based on four pillars at the heart of TekWissen’s business practices: transparency, prioritizing customer needs, workplace diversity, and respecting customer information.  These are our ethics. They are followed as business objectives and fundamentals; they have become the DNA of our organization. Our goal is for all TekWissen's customers, consultants, and employees to be proud to be associated with us because we are ethical in our overall business operations.
